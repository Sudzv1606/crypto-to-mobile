About CashCrypto

A personal project dedicated to providing estimated cryptocurrency conversion rates for informational use.

Our Story

CashCrypto was created by SudzV as a personal passion project to explore the intersection of cryptocurrency and mobile money services. Launched in 2025, this tool aims to offer users in regions like Kenya, Nigeria, the Philippines, and beyond a way to estimate conversion rates for BTC and USDT to popular mobile money platforms such as M-Pesa, Nigerian Naira, GCash, Airtel Money, and Vodacom M-Pesa. It’s built with a commitment to transparency, using real-time data from the CoinGecko API, updated every 5 minutes.

This is not a financial service or exchange platform—its sole purpose is to provide educational estimates. I developed it to share my interest in crypto technology and help others understand potential conversion values before engaging with actual exchange services.

My Mission

As the creator, my goal is to empower users with information about cryptocurrency conversions to mobile money. This project reflects my curiosity about how digital currencies can integrate with everyday financial tools in emerging markets. By offering free, real-time estimates, I hope to assist individuals in making informed decisions, while emphasizing that these are not guaranteed rates due to market volatility and exchange fees.

This is a solo endeavor, and I welcome feedback to improve the tool. Feel free to reach out through the contact options below or via my GitHub profile.
